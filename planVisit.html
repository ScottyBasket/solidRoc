Welcome to Solid Rock Church!

Who we are

We are formerly Brighton Presbyterian Church. After being part of two large denominations we’ve recently felt called out of the large denominational church structure. We are now an independent nondenominational church under the covering of Mutual Faith Ministries and Network under be leadership of Jeff and Nancy Clark.We are a church of new beginnings. Although we are currently small in numbers, we are large in passion for sharing the life changing message of Jesus Christ. We invite you to join us in this grassroots faith journey!

A Community of Imperfect but forgiven people

With diverse stories, from many backgrounds

Wrestling with life

Sharing a journey of faith

Loved by God

Every service you can expect:

-Starts at 9:15am and goes until 10:45am

-After service we have coffee and fellowship

-Adult Life Group goes from 11:00am to Noon

Style

You’ll find a mix of contemporary worship songs and hymns we offer gospel centered preaching and teaching. We have a time of prayer during the service, as well as opportunity of you to receive personal prayer at the close of the service

Where to Go

We are located at 1775 East Ave in Rochester, directly across from the East Ave Wegmans. Part in the front lot and enter through red doors. For fully accessible park in the rear and come through the back door
