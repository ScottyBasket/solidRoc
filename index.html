Other groups that meet here

We aren’t meant to do life alone. Find your own unique church family here: four congregations worship here each Sunday! Solid Rock Church is the proprietor of this beautiful property and is blessed to share it with many Gospel-centered churches and ministries. Although we may belong to different denominations, we are driven by a common goal: to love and serve the Lord Jesus Christ in all we do, and to love others.


Freechurch

Sunday at 11am in the Sanctuary

Our vision is to Love God, Love Others, and Love Life. We live out that vision through modern worship, a fun kids program, and huge events! Join us each week to worship God and hear relevant messages from Pastor Troy Bassett. We hope to see you there!

The Lamp Community Church

Sunday at 11am in the lower blossom room

Founding Bishop Daniel Soto, his wife Joy, and the TLC family are a Jesus-following, mission-leading, church-planting body of Christ, and a serving, people-loving, Bible-preaching ministry. Learn more about The Lamp Community Church on its Facebook page.

Bethel Express

Saturdays 10am - 3pm

BE is a ministry dedicated to affecting the lives of children primarily between the ages of 6 and 12 years of age with the Good news of Jesus Christ. Each Saturday, BE offers students a hot and nutritious breakfast, fellowship, and a worship experience with sound Biblical teaching.


Support Groups

Recognizing the challenges of addiction and the great need in our community, 1775 East Ave is committed to making meeting spaces affordable for legitimate support groups, led by experienced leaders.

Heroin Anonymous

Heroin Anonymous is a non-profit fellowship of men and women who have found a solution to heroin addiction. It meets in the lower level on Wednesdays at 7 pm and has a private entrance. HA is a fellowship of complete abstinence from all drugs and alcohol. We are recovered heroin addicts who meet regularly to help each other stay sober. The only requirement for membership is a desire to stop suffering from heroin addiction. There are no dues or fees for HA membership.

Big Book AA

Big Book meets each Sunday evening at 6 pm in the lower level. Private entrance is located at the back side behind the laundromat. There is plenty of parking, and 1775 East Ave is on bus line (stops are at Wegmans and Winton/East Ave.).

About Us

We are formerly Brighton Presbyterian Church. After being part of two large denominations we’ve recently felt called out of the large denominational church structure. We are now an independent nondenominational church under the covering of Mutual Faith Ministries and Network under be leadership of Jeff and Nancy Clark.We are a church of new beginnings. Although we are currently small in numbers, we are large in passion for sharing the life changing message of Jesus Christ.We invite you to join us in this grassroots faith journey!

What We Belive

  1. We Believe the Bible is inspired by God, infallible, and authoritative.
  2. We believe in one eternal God who exists as three separate persons; the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  3. We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, born of a virgin, lived a sinless life as He walked among men, demonstrated the authority and power of God in works and speech, died on the cross, rose from the dead on the third day. and is now seated at the right hand of God having accomplished all that is necessary for man’s salvation.
  4. We believe it is essential for man to repent of sin and by faith receive the finished work of Christ by confessing Him as Lord with his mouth and believing in his heart resulting in regeneration by the Holy Spirit.
  5. We believe the Holy Spirit is continuing the work He started at Pentecost empowering believers to live a godly life and continue in all the works of Jesus.
  6. We believe in the imminent return of Jesus and that those who have believed in Him will be resurrected to a heavenly dwelling in an incorruptible body, and those who do not believe will join Satan and his host in everlasting punishment.
  7. We believe the true Church is composed of all born-again believers in Christ, regardless of denominational affiliation.
  8. We believe all born-again believers have been commissioned to share the gospel of Jesus Christ to all the world.
